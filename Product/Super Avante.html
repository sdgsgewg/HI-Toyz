Super Avante Jr. (VZ)

5 | 3 reviews

Stock: Ready

Description

This assembly kit creates the Super Avante Jr., a shaft-driven Mini 4WD car with its own body based on the 1/10 scale Super Avante R/C model's; both have been designed by Mr. Kota Nezu of znug design. There is plenty to catch the eye, from depictions of the independent fenders to a molded image of the R/C model's inboard suspension. Canopy and nose window parts can be removed to give the model a vastly different look. The Super Avante Jr. is based upon the VZ chassis, using yellow 12-spoke wheels and low-profile tires, while markings are taken care of by the included metallic stickers.


Product Details:

  • Length: 158mm
  • Width: 98mm
  • Height: 38mm
  • Includes motor
  • Easy snap-together assembly
